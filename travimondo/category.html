Welcome to Ekebergrestauranten
Experience an architectural masterpiece in Bilbao, visit Pradas own art gallery in Milan or a glass vernissagevin in trendy and personal gallery in Berlin. For those interested in culture, Europe is a real goldmine of exciting contrasts. Each country has its history and arts tradition. In Belgrade, one can go on the barge club and dance to turbo-folk music genre that mixes traditional folk music with electronic beats. On the amphitheater in Croatian Pula given ballet performances under the open sky. The Netherlands is an exciting country for all design and architecture enthusiasts. We tip of nightclubs and bars in cities like Hamburg, Berlin, Barcelona, Belgrade, Budapest and Prague, to name a few. In Budapest you can drink cocktails in ruin bars and there is also the A38, a floating bar that has been named the best in the world by Lonely Planet. Fun family entertainment are abundant throughout Europe: day trips to the zoo, water park exotic and exciting theme parks. From the famous Tivoli Gardens in Denmark and Disneyland Paris to lesser known parks like Gardaland in Italy.
