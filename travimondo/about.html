Travimondo - Plan it . Book it. Do it

Have you ever thought how it could be to feel freedom, to feel sand on your feet and wind that blows your hair, to smell the coniferous forest in the morning, to hear raindrops... With all the pressures of modern life it’s easy to forget how incredible and mind-blowing our world is. What can bring you back these feelings and emotions? In Travimondo we strive to create authentic and meaningful travel experience that bring you to wonders both hidden and celebrated around the globe. There are so many wondrous destinations to discover places that are hard to believe exist at all. Our trips aim to do just that: to inspire a thirst for discovery and a passion for the wonders of the world.

Our Mission Statement

"Travimondo is not only a road trip planner, it is also the website that offer you might need for the trip. We are happy when we’re able to come up with ideas that can get people excited. We believe that our approach to what we do makes us different. We believe that traveling is one of the most important things in life."
